## Architecture types
### Box interiors (4 walls + furniture does the job):
- Everyday/domestic — house, bedroom, basement, kitchen, tavern, inn room, shop front
- Commerce/craft — blacksmith, market stall, apothecary, bakery, tannery, tailor, general store
- Criminal/hidden — thieves' den, fighting pit, opium den, gambling hall, secret meeting room
- Utility/infrastructure — warehouse, cellar, stable, mill
- Parts of Knowledge — wizard's study, cartographer room
- Parts of Wilderness — abandoned cabin, camp tent

### Architectural interiors (columns, arches, vaults, alcoves — geometry IS the space):
- Sacred/spiritual — temple, shrine, crypt, monastery hall, oracle chamber, ritual room
- Authority/power — throne room, jail cells, courthouse, war room
- Parts of Knowledge — library (tall shelves, balconies), archive vault, alchemist lab (could go either way)
- Underground — cave (organic walls), mine shaft (timber supports, irregular), sewer tunnel (arched), catacomb (alcoves), underground lake
